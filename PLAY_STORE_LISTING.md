## Graphic assets you still need to create

Play Store requires these images (Play Console will block publishing without them):

| Asset | Size | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **App icon** | 512 × 512 px | PNG, 32-bit. You already have a launcher icon — export it at this size. |
| **Feature graphic** | 1024 × 500 px | Banner shown at top of listing. Store name + tagline on a branded background. |
| **Phone screenshots** | min 2, up to 8 | 16:9 or 9:16. Screenshot your app: home, product detail, cart, checkout, order tracking. Just take them on your phone. |

Tip: You can make the icon and feature graphic quickly in Canva (free) using your blue storefront branding. For screenshots, run the app on your phone and capture the best-looking screens.
